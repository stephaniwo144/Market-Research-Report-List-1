<p><strong>The Premium Tequila Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blanco</li><li>Reposado</li><li>Joven</li><li>Anejo</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The premium tequila market is categorized into several types based on aging processes. Blanco tequila, also known as silver or white, is unaged and bottled immediately after distillation, showcasing pure agave flavors. Reposado is aged in oak barrels for 2 to 12 months, which imparts subtle wood and vanilla notes. Joven, or “young” tequila, blends blanco and aged tequilas for a balanced profile. Añejo is aged for 1 to 3 years, offering rich complexity. Other market types may include Extra Añejo, aged over 3 years, and specialty or flavored tequilas.</p></p>
