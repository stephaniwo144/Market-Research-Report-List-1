<p><p>The semi-conductive water-blocking tape market is categorized into single-sided and double-sided tapes. Single-sided tape features an adhesive layer on one side, ideal for applications requiring secure adhesion to a single surface, typically used in cable jointing and insulation. In contrast, double-sided tape has adhesive on both sides, providing enhanced bonding capabilities and versatility, often used for more complex installations where both surfaces need to be joined. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing electrical safety and water resistance in various industries.</p></p>
