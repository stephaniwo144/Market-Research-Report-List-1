<p><ul><li>Modular Ethernet switches</li><li>Fixed Configuration Ethernet switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Scalable Data Center Wired Switch market comprises two main types: modular Ethernet switches and fixed configuration Ethernet switches. Modular Ethernet switches provide flexibility, allowing for the addition of various modules to adapt to changing network demands. They are ideal for large data centers requiring extensive scalability. In contrast, fixed configuration Ethernet switches offer a set number of ports and features, making them more straightforward and cost-effective for smaller networks. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing data center performance and connectivity.</p></p>
