<p><p>A Ball Screw Actuator is a precision linear motion system that converts rotational motion into linear motion using a ball screw mechanism. The growing emphasis on energy efficiency and sustainable practices is also propelling market growth, as ball screw actuators are known for their lower power consumption compared to traditional systems.
