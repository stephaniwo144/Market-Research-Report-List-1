<p><ul><li>Inductive Couplers</li><li>Capacitive Couplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The contactless coupler market consists primarily of inductive and capacitive couplers. Inductive couplers transfer energy through magnetic fields, allowing for efficient power transfer over short distances, often used in applications like wireless charging and RFID systems. Capacitive couplers, on the other hand, utilize electric fields to transmit data or power, typically favored in low-power applications such as near-field communication (NFC). Both types enable seamless connectivity while minimizing physical connections and enhancing convenience in various technological applications.</p></p>
