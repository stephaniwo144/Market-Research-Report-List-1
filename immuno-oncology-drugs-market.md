<p><p>The immuno-oncology drugs market comprises several key types. Immune checkpoint inhibitors block proteins that inhibit immune responses, allowing the immune system to target cancer cells effectively. Immune system modulators enhance the overall immune response to tumors. Cancer vaccines stimulate the immune system to recognize and attack cancer cells. Oncolytic viruses are engineered to selectively infect and destroy cancer cells while stimulating an immune response. Other therapies may include monoclonal antibodies and adoptive cell transfer techniques, expanding treatment options.</p></p>
