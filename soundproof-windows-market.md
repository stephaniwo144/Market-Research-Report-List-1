<p><p>Soundproof windows primarily utilize two market types: laminated glass and vacuum glass. Laminated glass consists of multiple glass layers fused with a special interlayer, effectively dampening sound and enhancing security. Vacuum glass features an insulating space between two glass panes, minimizing sound transmission while providing excellent thermal insulation. Both options cater to residential and commercial needs, offering significant noise reduction, energy efficiency, and improved comfort, making them popular choices for urban environments and noisy surroundings.</p></p>
