Natural gas water heaters are popular for their efficiency and cost-effectiveness, using natural gas from the pipeline. Propane heaters are typically used in areas without natural gas access, offering similar benefits but requiring propane tanks. Oil-fired water heaters, while less common, are used in regions where oil is readily available, providing robust heating capabilities but often at higher operational costs.</p></p>
