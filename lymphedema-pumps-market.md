<p><p>Lymphedema pumps, also known as pneumatic compression devices, are therapeutic devices designed to manage lymphedema, a condition characterized by the accumulation of lymphatic fluid, often leading to swelling in limbs. These pumps work by providing intermittent compression to the affected area, promoting fluid drainage and reducing swelling. The technology plays a crucial role in enhancing the quality of life for patients with chronic conditions, including those recovering from surgery or dealing with cancer-related lymphedema.</p><p>The lymphedema pumps market is poised for significant growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 6.8% during the forecast period.
