<p><p>The passivation chemicals market primarily includes nitric acid and citric acid, both essential for enhancing the corrosion resistance of metals. Nitric acid is widely used for passivating stainless steel, effectively removing free iron and promoting a protective oxide layer. Citric acid, a more environmentally friendly alternative, is utilized for its chelating properties, especially in lightly alloyed or sensitive materials. Both types are crucial in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and food processing, ensuring the longevity and durability of metal components.</p></p>
