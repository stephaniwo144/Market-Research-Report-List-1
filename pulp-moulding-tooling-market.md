<p><p>The Pulp Moulding Tooling Market is primarily divided into two types: Thermoformed Pulp and Processed Pulp. Thermoformed Pulp involves heating pulp material to create molded shapes, offering durability and moisture resistance, ideal for packaging. Processed Pulp, on the other hand, includes mechanically or chemically treated pulp, allowing for enhanced strength and refinement, suitable for various specific applications such as consumer goods and protective packaging. Both types focus on sustainability, using renewable resources for eco-friendly solutions.</p></p>
