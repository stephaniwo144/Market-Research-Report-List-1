<p><ul><li>Siphon-style Gravel Cleaner</li><li>Electric Gravel Cleaner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gravel cleaner market features two primary types: siphon-style and electric gravel cleaners. Siphon-style gravel cleaners use a manual vacuum action to remove debris and waste from aquarium substrates, relying on gravity and water flow. They are favored for their simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, electric gravel cleaners utilize power-driven suction mechanisms, making them more efficient for larger tanks. These devices often come with additional features, such as built-in filters, to enhance their cleaning capabilities.</p></p>
