<p><p>The copper cable tester market can be divided into two primary types: analog and digital testers. Analog testers use traditional methods to measure signal quality and conductivity through needle gauges and basic visual indicators, offering simplicity and affordability. In contrast, digital testers utilize advanced technology to provide precise measurements, detailed diagnostics, and enhanced features like data logging and connectivity options. Digital testers are generally more sophisticated and capable of handling complex cabling systems, making them suitable for professional applications.</p></p>
