<p><p>The wind turbine friction material market comprises three main types: organic brake pads, sintered brake pads, and composite brake pads. Organic brake pads, made from natural materials, offer low wear and noise but may wear quickly. Sintered brake pads utilize metal powders, providing superior durability and heat resistance, ideal for high-stress applications. Composite brake pads combine materials to enhance performance and reduce weight, making them versatile for various environmental conditions. Each type serves specific operational needs within the wind turbine industry.</p></p>
