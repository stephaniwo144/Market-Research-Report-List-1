<p><ul><li>Waterproof VAE Emulsions</li><li>Ordinary VAE Emulsions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VAE emulsion market comprises two main types: waterproof and ordinary VAE emulsions. Waterproof VAE emulsions are formulated to resist water, making them ideal for applications requiring moisture protection, such as exterior paints and construction materials. Ordinary VAE emulsions, on the other hand, offer good adhesion and flexibility, suitable for indoor applications like paints and adhesives. Both types are crucial in industries such as construction, automotive, and textile, catering to specific performance needs based on environmental exposure.</p></p>
