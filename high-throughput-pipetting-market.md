<p><p>The high-throughput pipetting market comprises two primary types: manual and electric pipetting systems. Manual pipetting systems rely on the user's dexterity for accurate liquid transfer and are often more cost-effective, suitable for smaller labs or specific applications. In contrast, electric pipetting systems automate the pipetting process, enhancing precision and efficiency for high-volume tasks in research and clinical settings. The choice between these systems often hinges on factors like throughput requirements, budget constraints, and desired accuracy.</p></p>
