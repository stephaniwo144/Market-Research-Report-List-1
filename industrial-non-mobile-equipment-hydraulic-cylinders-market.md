<p><p>The industrial non-mobile equipment hydraulic cylinders market is primarily classified into two types: rod and telescopic cylinders. Rod cylinders feature a single, extendable rod that provides straightforward linear motion, making them suitable for various applications where space is not a constraint. In contrast, telescopic cylinders consist of multiple nested sections, allowing for greater extension while conserving space. This makes them ideal for applications requiring significant lifting height in limited areas, such as in material handling and loading equipment.</p></p>
