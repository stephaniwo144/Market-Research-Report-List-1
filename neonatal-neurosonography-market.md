<p><strong>The Neonatal Neurosonography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2D</li><li>3D</li><li>4D</li><li>Doppler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neonatal neurosonography market consists of several imaging modalities that help assess brain health in newborns. 2D neurosonography provides flat, two-dimensional images for diagnosing conditions like intraventricular hemorrhage. 3D neurosonography enhances visualization by offering three-dimensional representations, improving understanding of structural anomalies. 4D neurosonography adds the dimension of time, allowing for real-time visualization of brain movements. Doppler ultrasound evaluates blood flow within cerebral vessels, aiding in the diagnosis of vascular conditions. Each type plays a crucial role in neonatal brain assessment.</p></p>
