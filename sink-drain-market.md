<p><p>The **Pop-up sink drain** features a button or lever that raises or lowers the stopper, offering a sleek, modern design. The **Lift drain** uses a lift-and-turn mechanism, where the stopper is manually raised or lowered by twisting, ideal for traditional setups. The **Pull Out drain** has a chain or rod connected to the stopper, allowing it to be pulled up or pushed down, commonly found in vintage or classic sinks. Each type caters to different styles and functionalities in kitchen or bathroom sinks.</p></p>
