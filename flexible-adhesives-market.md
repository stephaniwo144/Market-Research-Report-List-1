<p><p>The flexible adhesives market consists of various types, primarily categorized into water-based, solvent-based, and others. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a solvent, offering low toxicity and easy cleanup, making them ideal for eco-friendly applications. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, providing strong bonding capabilities but often having higher volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions.
