<p><p>Milk centrifuge machines are categorized into two main types: hand-operated and motor-driven. Hand-operated centrifuges are typically used in smaller dairy operations or for home use, allowing users to manually separate cream from milk, requiring minimal investment. In contrast, motor-driven centrifuges are designed for larger-scale dairy production, offering higher efficiency and automation, enabling faster processing and better separation of milk components. Both types play essential roles in dairy processing, catering to different production scales and operational needs.</p></p>
