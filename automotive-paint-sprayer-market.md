<p><p>The automotive paint sprayer market comprises several types, primarily High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP), Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP), and others. HVLP sprayers deliver a high volume of airflow at low pressure, ensuring efficient paint transfer with minimal overspray, ideal for detailed work. LVLP sprayers use lower air volume and pressure, making them suitable for tight spaces and intricate applications. Other types might include airless and gravity-fed systems, catering to diverse user needs and preferences in automotive refinishing and restoration.</p></p>
