<p><ul><li>Bone Graft</li><li>Bone Graft Substitute</li><li>Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP)</li><li>BMAC (Bone Marrow Aspirate Concentrate)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spine Biologics market encompasses various types of products used for spinal surgeries and regenerative therapies. Bone grafts involve transplanting bone tissue to promote healing. Bone graft substitutes, often synthetic or biologically derived, serve similar purposes without harvesting natural bone. Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) utilizes concentrated platelets from blood to enhance healing. Bone Marrow Aspirate Concentrate (BMAC) combines stem cells and growth factors from bone marrow to facilitate tissue regeneration, making these solutions vital in spine surgery and recovery processes.</p></p>
