<p><p>The VOC Portable Gas Detector market features various types, including Photoionization Detectors (PIDs) and Gas Sensitive Semiconductors. PIDs utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules, effectively detecting volatile organic compounds at low concentrations, making them ideal for environmental monitoring and industrial safety. In contrast, Gas Sensitive Semiconductors operate by changing their electrical resistance in the presence of specific gases, offering quick responses and cost-effective solutions for detecting VOCs in various settings, including industrial and residential applications.</p></p>
