<p><p>Floor-standing automated biochemical analyzers are categorized into semi-automated and fully automated types. Semi-automated analyzers require some manual intervention, such as sample preparation and loading, while automating processes like reagent addition and measurement. In contrast, fully automated analyzers handle the entire workflow independently, from sample processing to reporting results, enhancing efficiency and throughput. These distinctions influence device selection based on laboratory needs, operational scale, and desired levels of automation to optimize diagnostic accuracy and laboratory productivity.</p></p>
