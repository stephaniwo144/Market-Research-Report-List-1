<p><ul><li>Plug and Perf</li><li>Sliding Sleeves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fracking water treatment systems are essential in managing wastewater generated during hydraulic fracturing. The Plug and Perf method involves using plugs and perforations to isolate specific sections of the well, allowing for targeted fracturing and more efficient water use. Conversely, Sliding Sleeves provide a more flexible approach, enabling operators to control the fracturing process dynamically as they can be opened and closed as needed. Both techniques contribute to optimizing resource recovery while minimizing environmental impacts.</p></p>
