<p><p>Carton overwrap films encompass two primary types: shrink films and stretch films. Shrink films are designed to tightly wrap around products, conforming to their shape when heat is applied, providing a secure and tamper-evident seal. In contrast, stretch films are engineered to be manually or machine-stretched around items, offering flexibility and strong containment without heating. Both types serve crucial roles in packaging, protecting goods during storage and transportation while enhancing presentation and product integrity in various industries.</p></p>
