<p><p>Single Wafer Processing Systems encompass several key market types essential for semiconductor manufacturing. Doping or ion implantation introduces impurities into wafers to modify electrical properties. Etching removes material to create intricate patterns, while deposition techniques, such as chemical vapor deposition or physical vapor deposition, add various materials onto wafers for device fabrication. Photolithographic patterning utilizes light to transfer designs onto the wafer surface for subsequent processing steps. Together, these processes are critical for the development of advanced semiconductor devices.</p></p>
