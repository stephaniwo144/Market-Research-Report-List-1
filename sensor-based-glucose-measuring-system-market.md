<p><p>The sensor-based glucose measuring system market is divided into two main types: self-monitoring glucose measuring systems and continuous glucose measuring systems. Self-monitoring systems allow individuals to measure their blood glucose levels using portable devices, providing instant feedback for managing diabetes. In contrast, continuous glucose measuring systems use wearable sensors to provide real-time glucose monitoring throughout the day, enabling better long-term glucose control and reducing the need for frequent finger pricks, thus improving the overall management of diabetes.</p></p>
