<p><p>The plastic healthcare and medical packaging market encompasses two main types: flexible and rigid packaging. Flexible packaging includes pouches, bags, and films, designed for easy manipulation and efficient space usage, often used for sterile medical devices and pharmaceuticals. Rigid packaging, on the other hand, involves containers like bottles, jars, and boxes that provide robust protection for medical products. Both types ensure safety, compliance with regulations, and extend the shelf life of healthcare products while maintaining sterility and integrity.</p></p>
