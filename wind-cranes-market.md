<p><strong>The Wind Cranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stiff Boom Cranes</li><li>Folding Boom Cranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind cranes market encompasses two primary types: stiff boom cranes and folding boom cranes. Stiff boom cranes feature a rigid, straight boom, providing high stability and lifting capacity, ideal for heavy-duty tasks. In contrast, folding boom cranes have a jointed design, allowing for greater flexibility and compact storage, making them suitable for operations in tight spaces. Both types serve essential roles in wind farm construction and maintenance, enhancing efficiency and safety in handling heavy equipment and materials.</p></p>
