<p><p>The flexible mechanical couplings market comprises three main types: sleeve, flanged, and clamped. Sleeve couplings connect shafts using a cylindrical sleeve, allowing angular or axial misalignment with minimal backlash. Flanged couplings utilize flanges fastened together, providing robust alignment and easier disassembly, ideal for high-torque applications. Clamped couplings feature a clamping mechanism that offers quick installation and reliable connections, accommodating variations in shaft size. These couplings help reduce vibration and enhance efficiency in various mechanical systems.</p></p>
