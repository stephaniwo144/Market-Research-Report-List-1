<p><p>PEEK gear market encompasses various types, including Spur, Helical, Bevel, and other specialized gears. Spur gears feature straight teeth and are efficient for transferring motion between parallel shafts. Helical gears have angled teeth, providing smoother operation and higher load capacity. Bevel gears enable the transfer of power between intersecting shafts, ideal for changing direction. Other market segments may include worm gears and ring gears, catering to specialized applications across industries like automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, leveraging PEEK's high-performance properties.</p></p>
