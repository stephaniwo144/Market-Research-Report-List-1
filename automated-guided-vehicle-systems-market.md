<p><p>Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) Systems are categorized based on navigation technologies. **Magnetic Guided AGVs** follow predefined paths using magnetic tapes or wires embedded in the floor, offering cost-effective and reliable navigation for repetitive tasks. **Laser Guided AGVs** use laser scanners and reflectors for precise, flexible navigation, ideal for dynamic environments requiring route changes. **Other AGV markets** include vision-guided, inertial-guided, or natural feature navigation systems, which leverage advanced sensors and AI for adaptability in complex, unstructured environments, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
