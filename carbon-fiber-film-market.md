<p><p>The carbon fiber film market is categorized into three primary types based on the wall structure: single-walled, double-walled, and multi-walled carbon fiber films. Single-walled films consist of a single layer of carbon atoms, offering high strength and lightweight properties. Double-walled films have two layers, enhancing structural integrity and flexibility. Multi-walled films contain several layers, providing superior thermal and electrical conductivity, making them suitable for advanced applications in aerospace, automotive, and electronics industries. Each variant serves unique functional requirements.</p></p>
