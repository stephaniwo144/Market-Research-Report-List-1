<p><p>The Open Surgery Heart Valves market primarily includes mechanical valves and Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement (TAVR) valves. Mechanical valves are durable, often made from materials like titanium or carbon, and are used in open-heart surgeries to replace damaged valves, requiring lifelong anticoagulation therapy. TAVR valves, on the other hand, offer a less invasive option for patients with aortic stenosis, allowing for implantation through catheters. This market caters to a growing demand for effective and innovative heart valve solutions, driven by an aging population and increased heart disease prevalence.</p></p>
