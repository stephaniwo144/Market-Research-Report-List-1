<p><p>The Air Bag Inflators Market comprises three main types: Pyrotechnic Inflators, Stored Gas Inflators, and Hybrid Inflators. Pyrotechnic Inflators use chemical reactions to generate gas rapidly, ensuring quick deployment. Stored Gas Inflators rely on pre-compressed gas, offering smoother and cooler inflation. Hybrid Inflators combine both methods, using a small pyrotechnic charge to release stored gas, balancing speed and temperature control. Each type caters to specific safety and performance needs, influencing their adoption across automotive applications.</p></p>
