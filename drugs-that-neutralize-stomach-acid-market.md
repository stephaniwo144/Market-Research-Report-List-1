<p><p>The market for drugs that neutralize stomach acid primarily includes H2 blockers and proton-pump inhibitors (PPIs). H2 blockers, such as ranitidine and famotidine, work by reducing the production of stomach acid, providing relief from conditions like GERD and ulcers. Proton-pump inhibitors, including omeprazole and lansoprazole, inhibit the proton pump in stomach lining cells, effectively reducing acid secretion for more prolonged relief. Both drug types are widely used for managing acid-related disorders and improving gastrointestinal health.</p></p>
