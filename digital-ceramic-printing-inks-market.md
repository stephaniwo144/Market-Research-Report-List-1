<p><p>The Digital Ceramic Printing Inks market is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I inks are typically water-based, offering lower environmental impact and increased safety during application, making them ideal for a variety of substrates. Type II inks, often solvent-based, provide enhanced adhesion and durability, suitable for high-performance applications in industrial settings. The choice between these types largely depends on the specific requirements of the printing process, substrate compatibility, and the desired finish of the printed ceramic products.</p></p>
