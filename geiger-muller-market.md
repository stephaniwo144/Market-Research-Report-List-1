Alpha particle detectors are designed for low-energy radiation, typically used in environments with alpha-emitting materials. Beta particle detectors measure higher-energy emissions and are crucial for monitoring radioactive substances. Gamma particle detectors address high-energy radiation, often from cosmic sources or nuclear materials.
