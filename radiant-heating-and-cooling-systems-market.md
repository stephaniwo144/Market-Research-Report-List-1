<p><ul><li>Water Media</li><li>Air Media</li><li>Electrical-Based Tubin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiant heating and cooling systems utilize different media to transfer thermal energy efficiently. Water media systems circulate warm or cool water through tubes embedded in floors or ceilings, providing uniform temperature control. Air media systems use air as the medium, distributing heated or cooled air through ductwork or vents, offering quick response times. Electrical-based tubing systems employ electric resistance or radiant electric heat cables, delivering effective thermal comfort with minimal space requirements, making them ideal for various applications and building designs.</p></p>
