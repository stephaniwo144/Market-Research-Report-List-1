<p><p>The aircraft scales market is segmented into two main types: platform scales and floor-standing scales. Platform scales are designed for weighing aircraft components and smaller items, providing portability and ease of use in various settings. In contrast, floor-standing scales are larger, often used in hangars or maintenance facilities, to accommodate heavier aircraft and provide more precise measurements. Both types play a crucial role in aviation safety, ensuring accurate weight assessments for maintenance and operational efficiency.</p></p>
