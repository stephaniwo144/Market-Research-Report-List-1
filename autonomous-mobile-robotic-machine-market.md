<p><p>The Autonomous Mobile Robotic Machine market is primarily divided into two types: LiDAR-based and LiDAR+Vision-based systems. LiDAR-based robots utilize laser scanning technology to create precise 3D maps of their environment, enabling effective navigation and obstacle avoidance. In contrast, LiDAR+Vision-based robots integrate visual data from cameras with LiDAR mapping to enhance perception and decision-making capabilities, allowing for more nuanced understanding of surroundings and improved operational efficiency in complex environments.</p></p>
