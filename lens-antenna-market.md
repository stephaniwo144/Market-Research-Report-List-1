<p><p>The lens antenna market includes various types, notably horn lens antennas and Luneberg lens antennas. Horn lens antennas combine the directional characteristics of horn antennas with the broad signal coverage of lenses, making them ideal for radar and communication applications. Luneberg lens antennas, on the other hand, utilize a spherical lens structure to achieve a wide beamwidth and excellent performance across multiple frequencies, often used in satellite communications and phased array systems. Both types enhance signal transmission and reception efficiency.</p></p>
