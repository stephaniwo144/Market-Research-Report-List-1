<p><ul><li>Bimetallic Thermometer</li><li>Glass Rod Thermometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial thermometer market features various types, including bimetallic and glass rod thermometers. Bimetallic thermometers use two metal strips that expand and contract at different rates, providing accurate temperature readings in various industrial applications. They are durable and suitable for harsh environments. Glass rod thermometers, on the other hand, utilize the expansion of liquid within a calibrated glass tube, offering precise measurements for lower temperature ranges. Both types cater to diverse industries, ensuring efficient temperature monitoring.</p></p>
