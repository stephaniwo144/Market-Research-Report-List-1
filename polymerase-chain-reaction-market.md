<p><p>The Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) market includes three key types: Real-Time PCR, Traditional PCR, and Digital PCR. Real-Time PCR enables the simultaneous amplification and quantification of DNA, making it highly efficient for various applications. Traditional PCR, the foundational technique, allows for DNA amplification but lacks real-time monitoring capabilities. Digital PCR offers precise quantification by partitioning samples into numerous individual reactions, enhancing sensitivity and accuracy. Together, these types cater to a wide range of research, diagnostic, and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
