<p><p>The diesel engine catalysts market includes several key types. Diesel Oxidation Catalysts (DOC) facilitate the oxidation of carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons, improving emissions. Catalyzed Diesel Particulate Filters (CDPF) combine filtration and catalysis to trap and reduce particulate matter. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) introduces a reductant, usually urea, to transform nitrogen oxides into harmless nitrogen and water. Other catalysts may include diverse technologies like lean NOx traps or advancing hybrid systems, enhancing overall engine efficiency and compliance with emissions regulations.</p></p>
