<p><ul><li>Drilled Carbon-ceramic Brake Discs</li><li>Slotted Carbon-ceramic Brake Discs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon-ceramic brake discs market is segmented into two main types: drilled and slotted carbon-ceramic brake discs. Drilled carbon-ceramic brake discs feature holes that enhance cooling and reduce weight, promoting effective heat dissipation during high-performance braking. Conversely, slotted carbon-ceramic brake discs have grooves that improve braking performance by allowing debris and gas to escape, thus increasing grip. Both types are favored in high-end vehicles for their superior thermal stability, lightweight properties, and enhanced performance characteristics under extreme conditions.</p></p>
