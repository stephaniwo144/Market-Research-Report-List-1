<p><p>The Bluetooth audio chips and modules market comprises two primary types: Ordinary Bluetooth audio chips and Audio System-on-Chip (SoC) solutions. Ordinary Bluetooth audio chips facilitate basic wireless audio transmission, ideal for simple applications requiring standard functionality. In contrast, Audio SoCs integrate multiple functions, including processing, amplification, and connectivity, into a single chip, enabling advanced audio performance and reducing the size and complexity of devices. This differentiation caters to various consumer needs, from basic earbuds to sophisticated audio systems.</p></p>
