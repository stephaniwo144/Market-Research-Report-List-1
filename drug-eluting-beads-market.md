<p><p>Drug Eluting Beads (DEBs) are innovative microspheres that are used in interventional oncology to deliver localized chemotherapy directly to tumors. These beads are composed of biocompatible materials that are coated or infused with anti-cancer drugs, allowing for sustained drug release over time. This targeted approach enhances therapeutic efficacy while minimizing systemic side effects, making DEBs a compelling solution for treating various malignancies, especially in patients who are not candidates for surgery.</p><p>The Drug Eluting Beads Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period. Factors contributing to this growth include an increasing incidence of cancer worldwide, advancements in medical technology, and the rising adoption of minimally invasive procedures. Furthermore, the growing preference for localized therapies over traditional systemic treatments is propelling the demand for DEBs. </p><p>Recent trends in the market include the development of next-generation DEBs that combine multiple therapeutic agents to enhance efficacy, as well as the integration of imaging techniques that aid in precise delivery. Additionally, the expansion of clinical applications, particularly in liver and colorectal cancers, is driving market expansion, making DEBs a pivotal area of interest in oncology therapeutics.</p></p>
