<p><p>Traction wheels are crucial components used in various transport systems, primarily trains and trams, for efficient movement. The market is segmented into Direct Current (DC) and Alternating Current (AC) types. DC traction wheels operate with direct current, offering advantages like lower maintenance and cost-effectiveness, making them ideal for urban rail systems. Conversely, AC traction wheels utilize alternating current, providing higher efficiency, scalability, and better performance over long distances, often favored in high-speed and freight rail applications.</p></p>
