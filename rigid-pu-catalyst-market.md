<p><strong>The Rigid PU Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-reactive Amine Catalysts</li><li>Reactive Amine Catalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rigid PU catalyst market consists of two main types: non-reactive and reactive amine catalysts. Non-reactive amine catalysts facilitate the polyurethanes' curing process without participating in the chemical reaction, providing stability and flexibility in production. Reactive amine catalysts, conversely, actively engage in the chemical reaction, accelerating the reaction rate and enhancing the final product's properties. Both types are essential in various applications, impacting the quality and performance of rigid polyurethane foams used in construction, automotive, and insulation sectors.</p></p>
