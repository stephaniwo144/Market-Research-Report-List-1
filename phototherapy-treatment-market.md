<p><p>Phototherapy treatment encompasses various types, primarily including conventional, LED, and fiberoptic markets. Conventional phototherapy employs fluorescent light tubes to treat skin disorders like psoriasis and eczema through UV radiation. LED phototherapy utilizes light-emitting diodes to target skin conditions with less heat and energy, promoting healing without harmful effects. Fiberoptic phototherapy delivers light through flexible fibers, allowing for targeted treatment in confined areas or specific tissues. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to diverse therapeutic needs in dermatology and other medical fields.</p></p>
