<p><p>Polymeric Ferric Sulfate (PFS) is an advanced coagulant used in water treatment and industrial applications. The market features several production methods. Hydrogen Peroxide Oxidation utilizes peroxide for oxidative polymerization, enhancing coagulation efficiency. Potassium Chlorate (Sodium) Oxidation involves chlorate as an oxidizing agent, creating a stable polymeric structure. Sodium Hypochlorite Oxidation Method employs hypochlorite to promote polymer formation, ensuring effective impurity removal. Each method influences the performance and application of PFS in treating water and wastewater.</p></p>
