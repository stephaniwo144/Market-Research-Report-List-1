<p><p>The Dissolved Air Flotation (DAF) unit market comprises two main types: Open Tank and Plate Pack systems. Open Tank DAF units feature a large, open basin where air is dissolved in water under pressure, allowing suspended solids to float to the surface for easy removal. In contrast, Plate Pack DAF units utilize inclined plates to enhance flotation efficiency by increasing the effective surface area for solid separation. Both types serve various applications in water treatment and industrial processes, offering distinct advantages based on space and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
