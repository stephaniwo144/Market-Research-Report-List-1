<p><strong>The Maritime Thermal Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uncooled Type</li><li>Cooled Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The maritime thermal camera market is primarily divided into two types: uncooled and cooled thermal cameras. Uncooled thermal cameras operate at ambient temperatures, utilizing microbolometer technology. They are more compact, energy-efficient, and less expensive, making them suitable for various marine applications. In contrast, cooled thermal cameras require cryogenic cooling to enhance image quality, offering superior resolution and sensitivity. These are typically used in high-performance situations, such as search and rescue operations or military applications, where detail and accuracy are vital.</p></p>
