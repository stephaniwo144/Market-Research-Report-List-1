<p><p>The All-In-One cloth diaper market is divided into two primary types: single-layer and multi-layer. Single-layer diapers consist of a single absorbent fabric layer sewn into the waterproof cover, making them easy to use and quick to dry. They are ideal for parents seeking simplicity. In contrast, multi-layer diapers feature several layers of absorbent materials, providing enhanced absorbency and better leak protection, although they may take longer to dry. Both types cater to different preferences for convenience and performance in diapering.</p></p>
