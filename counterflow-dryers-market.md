<p><p>Counterflow dryers are crucial in the drying process, featuring two primary types: batch dryers and continuous dryers. Batch dryers operate on a discrete cycle, processing a specific quantity of material at a time, making them ideal for small-scale operations or varied products. In contrast, continuous dryers facilitate a steady flow of material, allowing for uninterrupted drying, which increases efficiency in large-scale production settings. Both types leverage counterflow technology to enhance heat transfer and drying effectiveness.</p></p>
