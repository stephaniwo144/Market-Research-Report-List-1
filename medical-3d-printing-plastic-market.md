<p><p>The medical 3D printing plastic market encompasses various types, including ABS, PEEK, PETG, and others. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) is known for its toughness and ease of processing, making it suitable for prototypes. PEEK (Polyether Ether Ketone) is a high-performance polymer used for implants and surgical instruments due to its biocompatibility and chemical resistance. PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol-Modified) is favored for its clarity and durability, ideal for medical devices. Other materials may include Nylon and TPU, catering to diverse medical applications.</p></p>
