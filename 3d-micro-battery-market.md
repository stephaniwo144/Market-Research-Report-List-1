<p><ul><li>3D Interlaced MB</li><li>3D Concentric MB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Micro Battery market features two main types: 3D Interlaced Micro Batteries (MB) and 3D Concentric Micro Batteries (MB). 3D Interlaced MB integrates multiple electrodes in a complex interlaced structure, maximizing surface area and enhancing energy density. In contrast, 3D Concentric MB consists of layered cylindrical components, allowing for efficient charge/discharge cycles and thermal management. Both types cater to compact, high-performance applications, particularly in emerging technologies such as wearables, IoT devices, and microelectronics, driving innovation in energy storage solutions.</p></p>
