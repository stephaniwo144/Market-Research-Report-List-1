<p><p>The Laboratory Gas Pipelines Market features two primary types of switchover systems: semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic switchover systems require some manual intervention to switch gas supply sources, offering moderate automation and user control. In contrast, fully automatic switchover systems operate independently, detecting gas depletion and automatically transitioning to a backup source without user input. This leads to enhanced efficiency and safety in laboratory environments, ensuring a seamless and continuous gas supply for various applications.</p></p>
