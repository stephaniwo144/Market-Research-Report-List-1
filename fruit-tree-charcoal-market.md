<p><p>The fruit tree charcoal market can be categorized into lump charcoal and sugar charcoal. Lump charcoal is made from hardwoods and is favored for grilling due to its high heat output and minimal smoke. It is primarily used in barbecues and outdoor cooking. On the other hand, sugar charcoal, derived from the byproducts of sugarcane processing, is used in various applications, including activated carbon production and incense. Both types cater to different consumer preferences in cooking and industrial uses.</p></p>
