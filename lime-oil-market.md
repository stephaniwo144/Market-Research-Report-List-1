<p><strong>The Lime Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distilled</li><li>Expressed</li><li>Essence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lime oil is categorized into three main types based on extraction methods: distilled, expressed, and essence. Distilled lime oil is obtained through steam distillation of lime peels, resulting in a potent, concentrated oil. Expressed lime oil is extracted by cold pressing the fruit's rind, preserving the fresh aroma and flavor. Essence, on the other hand, often refers to synthetic or less concentrated lime oils, used primarily in food and fragrance applications. Each type serves distinct purposes in various industries.</p></p>
