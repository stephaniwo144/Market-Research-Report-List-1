<p><p>The Aluminum Pigment Powder market comprises two primary types: leafing and non-leafing aluminum pigments. Leafing aluminum pigment powder, characterized by its tendency to float and create a reflective surface, is often used in coatings and printing inks for high-gloss finishes. In contrast, non-leafing aluminum pigment powder remains evenly dispersed throughout the medium, providing a more muted appearance, making it ideal for applications such as plastics and automotive coatings where durability and color stability are essential.</p></p>
