Mn-SOD, present in mitochondria, is crucial in medical research for combating oxidative stress-related diseases. Fe-SOD, common in prokaryotes and plants, is utilized in agriculture and biotechnology for stress resistance. Each type serves distinct applications, driving demand across healthcare, cosmetics, and agriculture sectors.</p></p>
