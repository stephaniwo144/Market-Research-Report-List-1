<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Charging Station Transformer Market features two primary types: single-phase and three-phase transformers. Single-phase transformers are typically used in residential settings, providing sufficient power for home charging at lower costs. In contrast, three-phase transformers are designed for commercial and public charging stations, offering higher power capacity and faster charging speeds. This distinction caters to varying user needs, with single-phase being suitable for overnight charging and three-phase enabling rapid charging solutions for high-traffic locations.</p></p>
