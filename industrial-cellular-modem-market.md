<p><p>The industrial cellular modem market is primarily segmented into three types: NB-IoT LPWA technologies, LTE-M LPWA technologies, and other market variants. </p><p>NB-IoT, or Narrowband IoT, excels in providing extensive coverage and low power consumption, making it ideal for applications requiring long battery life. LTE-M, or LTE for Machine Type Communication, offers higher data rates and mobility support, suitable for mobile applications. The "other" category includes alternative technologies like 5G and legacy cellular standards, catering to diverse industrial use cases requiring varying bandwidth and connectivity options.</p></p>
