<p><ul><li>Point Detector</li><li>Open Path Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared combustible gas sensors market comprises two main types: point detectors and open path detectors. Point detectors are localized devices that monitor specific areas for gas emissions, offering quick response times and high sensitivity. They are ideal for confined spaces where leaks are concentrated. In contrast, open path detectors scan larger areas by measuring gas concentrations along a defined path, making them suitable for outdoor applications or expansive facilities. Both technologies ensure safety by enabling early detection of hazardous gases.</p></p>
