<p><strong>The Photoinitiator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free-radical Type Photoinitiator</li><li>Cationic Type Photoinitiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoinitiator market is primarily divided into two types: free-radical and cationic photoinitiators. Free-radical photoinitiators, when exposed to light, generate reactive free radicals that initiate polymerization, making them ideal for applications like inks and coatings. Cationic photoinitiators, on the other hand, produce cationic species upon light exposure, leading to a different polymerization mechanism suitable for various high-performance applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, influencing product performance and sustainability in photopolymer systems.</p></p>
