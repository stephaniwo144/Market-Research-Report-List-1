<p><strong>The Alternator Decoupler Pulleys (ADP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Overrunning Alternator Decoupler (OAD) Pulleys</li><li>One-Way Clutch (OWC) Pulleys</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alternator Decoupler Pulleys (ADP) play a crucial role in automotive systems by allowing the alternator to operate efficiently while reducing vibrations and stress on the drive belt. The Overrunning Alternator Decoupler (OAD) pulley enables the alternator to spin freely when the engine speed decreases, minimizing load on the engine. Conversely, One-Way Clutch (OWC) pulleys also allow for free rotation in one direction but are typically designed for specific applications where enhanced torque transfer is needed. Both types contribute to improved engine performance and longevity.</p></p>
