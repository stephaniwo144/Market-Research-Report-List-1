<p><p>The biodegradable freezer bags market comprises several key types. Poly Lactic Acid (PLA) bags are derived from renewable resources like corn starch, offering good clarity and rigidity. Polybutylene Succinate (PBS) offers flexibility and durability. Cellulose-based bags are made from plant fibers, providing a natural alternative.
