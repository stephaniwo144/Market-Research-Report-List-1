<p><p>The **Concrete Sleepers Market** includes **Reinforced Concrete Sleepers** and **Prestressed Reinforced Concrete Sleepers**. Reinforced Concrete Sleepers use steel bars for added strength, making them durable and cost-effective for railway tracks. Prestressed Reinforced Concrete Sleepers, on the other hand, are manufactured by applying tension to steel wires before concrete sets, enhancing load-bearing capacity and resistance to cracking. Both types are widely used in railway infrastructure, with prestressed sleepers being preferred for heavy-load applications due to their superior performance and longevity.</p></p>
