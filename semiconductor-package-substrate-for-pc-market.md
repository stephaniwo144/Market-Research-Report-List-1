Key types include FC-BGA (Flip-Chip Ball Grid Array), which offers high performance through direct chip connections; FC-CSP (Flip-Chip Chip Scale Package), known for its compact size; WB BGA (Wire Bond Ball Grid Array), recognized for versatility in larger packages; and WB CSP (Wire Bond Chip Scale Package), which balances size and performance. Each type serves specific applications, catering to evolving technological needs.</p></p>
