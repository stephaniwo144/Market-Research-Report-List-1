<p><strong>The ELISA Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Filter ELISA Analyzers</li><li>Optical Grating ELISA Analyzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ELISA analyzers market comprises two main types: Optical Filter ELISA Analyzers and Optical Grating ELISA Analyzers. Optical Filter ELISA Analyzers utilize specific optical filters to isolate wavelengths for detecting light emitted during the assay, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, Optical Grating ELISA Analyzers employ diffraction gratings to separate light into its component wavelengths, allowing for more precise measurements and greater sensitivity. Both technologies are essential for effective enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays in clinical diagnostics and research.</p></p>
