<p><p>The fiberglass building products market comprises three main types: CFRP (Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer), GFRP (Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer), and other variants. CFRP is known for its strength and lightweight properties, making it ideal for high-performance applications. GFRP, combining durability with corrosion resistance, is commonly used in construction, infrastructure, and automotive sectors. Other market types include aramid fiber composites and natural fiber-reinforced polymers, catering to diverse applications in sustainable building and specialized industrial uses, enhancing overall performance and functionality.</p></p>
