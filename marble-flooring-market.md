<p><p>The marble flooring market is segmented into three main types: Dolomite, Magnesium, and Calcite. Magnesium marble offers unique aesthetic qualities with a range of colors and veining, appealing to luxury markets. Calcite marble, characterized by its crystalline structure, is prized for its translucence and elegance, often used in upscale residential and commercial applications. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and architectural needs.</p></p>
