<p><ul><li>Thermostatic</li><li>Self-Actuating</li><li>Internally Sensed</li><li>Electrically or Pneumatically Actuated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature control valves market encompasses several types, each serving specific applications. Thermostatic valves automatically adjust based on temperature changes, ensuring optimal thermal regulation. Self-actuating valves operate without external power, responding directly to process conditions. Internally sensed valves measure temperature within the system, enhancing precision. Electrically or pneumatically actuated valves rely on external signals for adjustments, offering enhanced control and responsiveness. Together, these valve types cater to diverse industrial needs, improving efficiency and system performance.</p></p>
